Join Us for Two Transformative Weekends of Mission and Ministry

Are you ready to deepen your understanding of global missions and how you can live a life driven by it? At the "GO" Mission Weekend, (1 & 2 November), we invite you to embark on a journey that will challenge, inspire, and equip you for the Great Commission.

During this event, led by David Riddell, a leading voice in ministry and counselling, we will focus on the topic of "Sustainable Mission". You'll gain practical tools to ensure your mission impacts lives today and lasts for generations. Topics include balancing personal life and ministry, mental health, relationships, and finances.

Whether you're new to missions or seeking deeper wisdom to continue your work, this weekend will leave you spiritually enriched, practically equipped, and ready to take your place in God’s unfolding plan for the nations.

Global Mission School

Global Mission School is a seven-week (25 hours) training designed to awaken you to God's big-picture purpose, His deep love for all nations, and His desire to use His people to be a blessing on this earth. Through weekly lectures, you will learn how God has been advancing His kingdom throughout history. You will be inspired and challenged to actively engage in God's mission of bringing all nations back to Him. These seven weeks will transform your worldview, helping you recognize that you have been blessed to be a blessing to all nations.

About Us

Frontier Nations is a missions coalition made up of churches, individuals and mission organizations united by the common goal of making disciples of nations within the 10/40 Window, where 96% of all unreached people of the world reside. We concentrate our efforts on this region, partnering with the global church to bring the good news to those who have never heard it.
